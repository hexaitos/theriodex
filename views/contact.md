# Contact Us

You found a problem, have ideas for improving Theriodex or just want to chat? Then you can send us an email to [contact@theriodex.net](mailto:contact@theriodex.net). If you know how to work with Git and have an account on Codeberg, you can also open an issue on Theriodex’s [Codeberg page](https://codeberg.org/hexaitos/theriodex) if you have found a problem you would like to report or have an idea for improving Theriodex.
